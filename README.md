## About

The **Market Viewer App** is a component of Market Viewer ecosystem. Its primary purpose is to act as a remote control and configuration hub for your physical Market Viewer device.

This app allows you to connect directly to your self-hosted (or the default hosted by us) [market viewer api](https://github.com/market-viewer/market-viewer-api) and manage what your devices display.

## Features

* **Device management:** Manage all your Market Viewer devices.
* **Screen Configuration:** Create and customize the screens of your Market Viewer devices.
* **Self-Hosted Ready:** Point the app to your own personal backend instance with a custom URL.
* **Notifications:** Recieve notifications when your server is offline
* **Native Android Design:** Native Android experience featuring Dark/Light Mode and Android 12+ Dynamic Wallpaper Colors.

## Download & Installation

You can get the Market Viewer App through the following channels:

### GitHub Releases
You can download the latest standalone `.apk` directly from [Releases Page](https://github.com/market-viewer/android-client/releases/tag/first-release).

## Quick Start

1. Download and open the app.
2. Tap the **Server Settings** icon on the login screen.
3. Enter the URL of your self-hosted Market Viewer backend.
4. Log in and start configuring your screens!
